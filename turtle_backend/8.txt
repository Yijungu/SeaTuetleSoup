Jake typically scores below 50 out of a possible 100 on his tests, and to avoid getting scolded by his mom, he has been lying to her that the tests are out of 50 points. However, on this test, the answers he guessed aligned so well that he ended up scoring more than 50. Jake fears that he may have inadvertently revealed that the total score for the test is not 50, but 100, since he scored more than 50 this time. If his mom discovers his longstanding lie, Jake will be in serious trouble. This prospect frightened Jake.
