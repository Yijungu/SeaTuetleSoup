Annie lived near the bookstore. Once Annie is house was peaceful. But the peace was broken when the construction of a bookstore to be built next to Annie is house began. The noise at the construction site caused Annie great inconvenience.
But one day, Annie heard that a bookstore near her house was opening soon. This meant that the construction of the bookstore would be completed in the near future. Annie was very happy to hear the news that the noise of the construction would disappear.
 After the construction, Annie forgot the bookstore and never went to it.
