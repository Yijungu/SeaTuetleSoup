Chloe was playing the board game "Burmavel" with her friends, and the game was soon coming to an end. Chloe's friend had invested a lot of money and built a hotel on a space right in front of her. If she rolled the dice incorrectly, she could end up going a few more steps and lose all the money she had at that hotel. Chloe was agonizing over what she should do in the face of this crisis.
