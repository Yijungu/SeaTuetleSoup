Lucas was a lighthouse keeper. But one night, he inadvertently forgot his duty and did not turn on the light of the lighthouse. That night, as he spends his time comfortably watching television, he receives shocking on the news that several ships have been wrecked at sea and people died due to his careless behavior. He immediately turned on the light of the lighthouse, but it was too late. As a result, Lucas, who suffered from heavy guilt, eventually gave up his life without enduring the sadness.
